Hacking Grunt Video Series

This repository roughly contains step by step commits from the Hacking Grunt Video Series. In this video series, Rami Sayar shows you how to quickly hack together a Gruntfile. The video series starts off with an introduction of Grunt, how to install it and how to add various task runners to your Gruntfile including CSS minifiers, SASS compilation, bower integration, uglifying JavaScript and more...
